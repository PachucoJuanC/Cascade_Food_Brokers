Rob Servoss

Account Executive

Rob started in the foodservice business in 1981 as a street sales associate with Pacific Gamble Robinson. Rob advanced to the roll of sales manager, buyer, and in 1986 Category Manager for the newly created Food Services of America. In 1995 Rob bought a small country grocery store in his home town of Mt. Vernon, WA and operated the store until 2003 when he went to work for Acosta Sales & Marketing until 2012. Rob then joined Cascade Food Brokers where he is currently employed as a sales associate covering Western Washington, Wenatchee & Yakima.

Louie Agosto

Account Executive

Louie Agosto has been working in the meat business for much of his life. A meat cutter with 20 years’ experience, he knows his way around the meat counter and beyond. Starting off his career at Ray’s Food Place was the perfect launching pad for Louie. He advanced through the ranks to ultimately become the Meat Manager of one of the busiest stores in the chain. It’s that same depth of knowledge and dedication that he brings to the food broker world as the Southern Oregon Account Executive for Cascade Food Brokers.

Jodie Wallerstedt

Account Executive

Growing our clients and sales is her top priority. She truly believes all for one and one for all- It takes a team to reach great success.
